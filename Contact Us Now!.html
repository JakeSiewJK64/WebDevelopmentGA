Evolto Tea and Coffee are dedicated to serve our customers with the best and freshly brewed tea and coffee that we guarantee will soothe any bitter day. Evolto Tea and Coffee was founded by three college students as part of a college project in 2019, fortunately it was a project that thrived and became one of Malaysia's top coffee and tea house available.




Jake Siew
Founder of Business

Jake presented the idea of opening a coffee and tea house to cater to people who enjoy the atmosphere of being in a coffee and tea house and enjoy rich and freshly brewed coffee and tea.


Jia Ming
Co-Founder of Business

Jia Ming supported the idea of the coffee and tea house idea and provided much aid to making the idea possible. Jia Ming was responsible in designing Evolto's Coffee and Tea's website in order to entice our customers and inventing the order system to bring the coffee and tea house to our customers.


Ze Zhang
Co-Founder of Business

Ze Zhang also helped designed Evolto's Coffee and Tea's website to function so that our customers are provided the functionality to register an account and log into our website to receive promotions and discounts for their purchase.
