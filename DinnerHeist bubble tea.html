We at Evolto's Tea and Coffee assures you that the ingredients we use in making our beverages were obtained from certified and legitimate sources to ensure that our beverages are natural and safe to consume.

※~Our Bubble Tea ~※

 

Evolto's Tea and Coffee use some of the finest tea ingredients such as 100% natural tea powder and handmade tapioca pearls that bubble tea lovers cannot get enough of. Evolto promises that how your beverage tastes were naturally inplemented.



※~Our Tea Leaves~※

Evolto's Tea and Coffee procure our tea leaves from Cameron Highland, Malaysia's main tea plantation as well as from overseas. Our tea leaves vary from location to location thus making our tea truly exotic and special in its own way.


※~Our Coffee Beans~※

Evolto's Tea and Coffee imports its coffee beans all the way from Brazil, one of the world's leading coffee bean producer.


※~Our non-caffeine sources~※

Besides tea and coffee we're best known for, our other menu offers alternatives to those who are less interested in caffeinated beverages. We at Evolto import our chocolates to make our chocolate drinks to promise that creamy goodness.


※~Our Smoothies~※

We at Evolto use the freshest fruits you can find locally to concoct one of our savory smoothies. Despite our reputation for good tea and coffee, we at Evolto also take pride in our smoothies to cater those who are looking forward to a fruitful day!
